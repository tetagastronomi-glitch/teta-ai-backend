FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What AI providers do you support? +

We support Anthropic (Claude), OpenAI (GPT-4), and Google Gemini. You choose your provider during setup and can switch anytime. You bring your own API key — costs are typically $2-5/month depending on volume.

Is there a monthly fee? +

No. Te Ta AI is a one-time purchase. The only recurring cost is your AI API usage (typically $2-5/month) and hosting (Railway free tier or ~$5/month). No hidden fees, no per-seat pricing, no surprises.

Can I use it with Instagram and Messenger? +

Yes! The Pro plan includes Instagram DM, Facebook Messenger, Web Chat widget, and Google Business Messages — all feeding into one unified inbox. The Starter plan includes WhatsApp only.

How long does setup take? +

About 5 minutes. Our setup wizard (guided by Jerry, your AI assistant) walks you through 3 simple steps: choose your AI provider, enter restaurant details, and connect WhatsApp. No coding required.

Do I need technical knowledge? +

No. We offer one-click deploy to Railway, Docker support, and a step-by-step setup wizard. If you can click a button, you can run Te Ta AI. Our INSTALL guide covers everything.

Can I use it for multiple restaurants? +

Yes, unlimited. The platform supports multi-tenant architecture. Each restaurant gets its own dashboard, settings, and bot. One license covers all your locations.
