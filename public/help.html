🚀 Getting Started

Welcome to Te Ta AI. Your AI Restaurant Manager (we call him Jerry) answers customer messages, takes reservations, sends you daily reports, and learns your restaurant's style over time.

Your first 10 minutes

  1. Open your dashboard using the PIN we sent you at activation.
  2. Go to Settings → WhatsApp and scan the QR code with your restaurant's WhatsApp.
  3. Message your own WhatsApp with "Hi" to see Jerry in action.
  4. Add any menu items or special notes under Settings → Restaurant Info.
💡 Tip Jerry gets smarter the more information you give him about your restaurant. Don't skip the description field — it's how he answers customer questions.

🔧 Setup

Connecting WhatsApp

  1. Go to Settings in your dashboard.
  2. Click the WhatsApp tab.
  3. A QR code will appear — open WhatsApp on the phone you want to use → Settings → Linked Devices → Link a Device.
  4. Scan the QR code. Connection is instant.
⚠️ Important Use a dedicated phone number for your restaurant, not your personal one. WhatsApp links one device per account.

Setting opening hours

Opening hours tell Jerry when he can accept reservations. Outside these hours, he'll politely decline and offer an alternative time. Update them anytime in Settings → Hours.

Your AI provider

Te Ta AI works with Claude, OpenAI, Gemini, Mistral, or Groq. You pay the AI provider directly for message costs (usually $1-5/month for a small restaurant). Your key lives encrypted on our server and is never shared.

☀️ Daily Use

Morning check (9 AM)

Every morning Jerry sends you a WhatsApp summary: reservations for today, overnight messages, and anything unusual that needs your attention. You can change the send time in Settings → Reports.

Real-time alerts

Jerry pings you immediately for: VIP guests, groups of 6+, allergen requests, and booking conflicts. Turn any of these off in Settings → Notifications.

Managing reservations

  • Filter by date, status, or channel at the top of the dashboard.
  • Click any row to see the full conversation, notes, and guest history.
  • Confirm, reschedule, or cancel with one click — Jerry notifies the guest automatically.

Analytics

The Analytics page shows booking volume, no-show rate, busiest hours, and guest retention. Use it Monday mornings to plan your week.

🛟 Troubleshooting

Jerry isn't answering customers

  1. Check the WhatsApp status in Settings → WhatsApp. If it says "disconnected", scan the QR code again.
  2. Confirm your AI key is still valid — Settings → AI Provider → Test Key.
  3. Look at the live message log in Messages. If the message arrived but Jerry didn't reply, your AI provider may have rate-limited you.

Reservations aren't saving

Make sure your opening hours cover the requested time. If Jerry says "we're closed" at times you are actually open, update Settings → Hours.

Customer says they got a wrong answer

Add the correct information to Settings → Restaurant Info description. Jerry re-reads this on every message, so fixes take effect immediately.

Dashboard looks broken

Hard-refresh with Ctrl+F5 (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac). We push updates often — this forces your browser to load the latest version.
